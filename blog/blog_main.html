This is not a blog.

Posted on: 1/19/2016

I want this to be more than a simple internet blog. I want it to be a place where I can share the new things I learn about with the world, tweaks, tips, and some programming tutorials. Of course I will also rant about the current big problems and events in the world of technology but I want it to be more than just that. For example you may find me ranting about whenever a big tech company like steam messes up big time like they did just last Christmas. You may also find programming tutorials on making simple games and little tools. Sometimes I'll just find some really cool project on GitHub that doesn't have very many stars and I'll write about that. Maybe if I'm lucky this whole blogging thing will even improve my writing who knows.

- Kota
