Hidden: The Ghosthunter

A few years ago I started a 2D platformer called Hidden. Unfortunately I lost interest in it because the engine was not good enough for what I wanted to do. I now have plans of remaking it in a way with a different engine and a bit better designed story!

While it has it's bugs and is very short it's still a cool concept and fun to play the first few levels so check it out if you are interested or just check back for updates about me remaking the new version.
