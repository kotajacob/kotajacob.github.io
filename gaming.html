Help: You can either join an existing channel or create your own. If you create a channel, you can give it a password so that only your friends can join it. To create a password for your channel, right click your channel and click edit; then type your password into the box labeled 'Password'. To join a channel with a password, click the 'Server' button in the top left corner; then click the 'Access Tokens' button; next click the 'Add' button and type the channel's password; then join the channel.
