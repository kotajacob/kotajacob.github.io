Help: You can either join an existing channel or create your own. If you create a channel, you can give it a password so that only your friends can join it. To create a channel, right click on the root channel and click add; then type a name for your channel and a brief description. To create a password for your channel, right click your channel and click edit; then type your password into the box labeled 'Password'. To join a channel with a password, click the 'Server' button in the top left corner; then click the 'Access Tokens' button; next click the 'Add' button and type the channel's password; then join the channel.

If you want to join the normally off limits CSGO Channel either ask one of the csgo players for permission or put the name of the top NA team in lower case with numbers NOT written out in your tokens. To add a token go in Server>Access Tokens>Type it in and addd what you want.
