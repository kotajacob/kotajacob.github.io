Creatures of Darkness

Creatures of darkness is one of my older games but also one of the first ones I took seriously. Having made it in 9th grade it is a bit dated and I may remake it in an updated engine and with improved graphics in the future.

It is still a fun little arcade game to play and compete to get the highest score in. If the controls feel a little strange you may want to change them to Alex, one of the beta testers, favorite controls as they are a little easier to learn for some people.
